Attractions in Loni

Asia’s First Co-operative Sugar Factory

The Pravara Co-operative Sugar Factory, established in 1950 in Loni (Pravaranagar), Maharashtra, is Asia’s first co-operative sugar factory. It was founded by Padmashree Dr. Vitthalrao Vikhe Patil with the aim of empowering farmers by making them owners and partners in production. This initiative marked the beginning of the co-operative movement in India, helping to improve rural livelihoods through shared profits, education, healthcare, and infrastructure. The factory became a model for rural development and inspired many similar co-operatives across India.

Pravara Rural Engineering College

Pravara Rural Engineering College (PREC), established in 1983 in Loni, Ahmednagar district, Maharashtra, is a premier institution under the Pravara Rural Education Society. Affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University and approved by AICTE, the college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines. With a commitment to rural development and technical excellence, PREC provides quality education and has a strong placement record, making it a notable engineering college in the region.

Pravara Medical Trust (PMT)

Pravara Medical Trust (PMT) is a pioneering healthcare and educational organization established in 1972 by Padmashri Dr. Vitthalrao Vikhe Patil in Loni, Ahmednagar district, Maharashtra. The trust has been instrumental in transforming rural healthcare and education in India.
