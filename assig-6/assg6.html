Welcome to the Loni city

Discover the Beauty of Loni

Loni also known as Loni-pravara is a town in Rahata taluka, Ahmednagar, Maharashtra, India. It is known for the biggest Shiv Jayanti Festival in the world. There are many colleges in this town.

Explore the rich culture, vibrant streets, and historical landmarks of Loni city.

Join us in experiencing the unique charm and hospitality of this beautiful city.
